Emmys

The Emmy Awards, or simply Emmy, is the largest and most prestigious award given to Series and television professionals. It is presented at several annual events held throughout the year, each honoring one of the various sectors of the television industry. The two events that receive The biggest media coverage is the Primetime Emmys, where awards such as "Best Drama Series" take place, and the Daytime Emmy Awards that recognize outstanding work in American primetime programming and entertainment daytime, respectively. There are also specific awards, with their own ceremonies, for programming aimed at children and families (Children's & Family Emmy Awards), sports (Sports Emmys), journalism (News and Documentary Emmys), technology (Technology and Engineering Emmys), regional programming (Regional Emmys) and school and university audiovisual production. Additionally, the International Emmy Awards are granted to television programming produced and initially shown outside the United States, the award for which usually occurs at the end of the year.

The name "Emmy" was used as a feminization of "immy", a nickname used for picture tubes that were easily found in early television cameras. The Statuette shows a winged woman holding an atom. The Emmy is considered one of the four biggest entertainment american awards , the others being the Grammy (music), the Oscar (cinema) and the Tony (theater).

There are three related but separate organizations present at the Emmy Awards: the Academy of Television Arts & Sciences (ATAS), the National Academy of Arts & Television Sciences (NATAS) and the International Academy of Television Arts and Sciences (IATAS). THE ATAS first awarded the Emmy in 1949 to honor programs produced in the Los Angeles area. Angeles before becoming a national event in the 1950s to honor programs aired in the entire country. Over the next two decades, ATAS, NATAS, and IATAS expanded the award to honor other areas of the TV industry, with each organization responsible for managing a specific set of Emmy competitions.
